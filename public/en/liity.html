Want to sing with us?

We welcome new singers in the beginning of every semester by organising open rehearsals and auditions in September and January. Prior experience on choir singing is not mandatory, but it is useful if you are be able to practice also on your own.

In the auditions, only the vocal range leaders will be present. You will be asked to sing one song of your choice without accompaniment, after which we will test your vocal range and do a melody repetition task.

Follow us on Facebook to hear about the auditions!
