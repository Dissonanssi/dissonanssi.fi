Spring Concert 2025

Our upcoming spring concert will take place on Sunday April 27, 2025, at Balderin sali (Aleksanterinkatu 12, Helsinki) starting at 7 PM.

The spring concert will celebrate the 160th anniversary of Sibelius and we will be singing arrangements composed by him. The program will also include works by other Finnish composers.

You are warmly welcome!

Dissonanssi concerts

Dissonanssi organises two concerts during the year, one during the spring semester and one during the fall semester. In addition to these, you can see us perform in some other events, for example Kuorojen kierros during Art Goes Kapakka. We post information of upcoming gigs in Facebook!
