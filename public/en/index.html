Dissonanssi

We are

Chamber choir Dissonanssi: A mixed choir from Otaniemi singing diverse music all the way from renaissance to glam metal — without forgetting to have fun!

The choir was founded in 2013. Our members are mainly students of Aalto University. The choir association works under Aalto University Student Union.

We have rehearsals once a week in Otaniemi, and we have 3-4 performances a year. The choir is conducted by Joonas Tuominen. Our rehearsals are conducted in Finnish, however you are welcome to join even with no Finnish speaking skills, someone is always willing to translate!
